# LocalFlow: Discord Infrastructure & Onboarding Flow

**Server Name:** LocalFlow Community
**Category:** 🛡️ Inner Circle (Private)

## 1. Channel Structure
- `#🏛-architects-manifesto`: Read-only. Houses the LocalFlow vision, NDL specs, and 10-year plan.
- `#💬-architect-lounge`: General discussion for the first 100 members.
- `#🛠-tool-integration-dev`: Technical deep-dives on the MCP bridge (Ollama, AppFlowy, NocoDB).
- `#🌊-workflow-showcase`: Where architects post their first canvas-based workflows.
- `#🆘-architect-support`: Direct line to the Accio team (fs-dev, devops, agent-engineer).

---

## 2. Onboarding Flow (Automated)
1.  **Invite Received:** Architect joins via unique one-time link.
2.  **Role Assignment:** Bot automatically assigns the `@Founding Architect` role.
3.  **Verification:** Architect must link their GitHub/X handle to unlock the `#🏛-architects-manifesto`.
4.  **Welcome Sequence:**
    - "Welcome, [Name]. You are node #[Count]/100. Check the manifesto to see why we're replacing the DOM with the NexusCanvas."
5.  **Call to Action:** "Head to `#🛠-tool-integration-dev` to see our current Ollama bridge spec."

---

## 3. Engagement Strategy
- **Weekly Architect Syncs:** 30-minute "No-meeting meetings" (async updates).
- **Bounty Board:** "First person to build an autonomous NocoDB-to-AppFlowy sync wins the first 'Workflow Master' badge."
